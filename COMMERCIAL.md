# Commercial Licensing

Organizations need a commercial license to use the BSL-licensed GroundControl server components in production when they are above either of the thresholds in the Additional Use Grant: US $1,000,000 or more in annual revenue or annual operating budget for the most recently completed fiscal year, or US $5,000,000 or more in cumulative equity funding received to date. A commercial license is also required — regardless of organization size — when the production use involves offering GroundControl to third parties on a hosted or embedded basis in order to compete with a paid version of the Licensed Work.

A commercial license grants the right to run the GroundControl server in production at the organization's actual size and operating scale. It does not include support, an SLA, priority bug fixes, or any other service commitment unless those are negotiated separately.

There are two pricing tiers:

- Organizations with US $1M to US $25M in revenue or operating budget
- Organizations above US $25M in revenue or operating budget

Pricing is determined per engagement based on organization size and use case. Contact for a quote.

To request a commercial license, email mrtaikandi@hotmail.com with your organization name, approximate revenue bracket, intended deployment scope (including the number of environments and regions), and any timeline requirements.

Commercial licensing is handled through a manual quote and contract process. There is no self-serve checkout.
